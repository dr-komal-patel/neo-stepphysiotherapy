Neo-Step is a trusted physiotherapy and rehabilitation centre in Pimple Saudagar, Pune — providing expert, personalised, evidence-based physiotherapy for neuro, spine, and orthopedic conditions.

"We treat every patient not through the lens of their diagnosis, but through the lens of their potential to fully recover."

Stroke RehabSpinal CordCerebral PalsyBell's PalsyParkinson'sGait TrainingHand TherapyOnline Consult

Neo-Step is a large, fully-equipped rehabilitation centre in Pimple Saudagar with all the specialist tools required for comprehensive neuro and orthopaedic physiotherapy — from gait training rigs and balance boards to electrotherapy and spasticity management.

Dr. Komal's approach goes well beyond standard physiotherapy. She understands the root cause of each condition and builds personalised programs focused on the patient's overall development — not just basic exercises. Her warm, approachable manner puts even the most anxious patients at ease from day one.

With online consultations now available, Neo-Step makes expert physiotherapy accessible to patients across India and NRIs worldwide, without needing to visit the clinic to get started.

Got a Question

Frequently Asked Questions

01. What conditions does Neo-Step treat?
+
Neo-Step provides comprehensive rehabilitation for neurological conditions (stroke, cerebral palsy, Parkinson's, Bell's palsy, TBI), spinal conditions (spinal cord injury, spondylitis, back pain), orthopedic and post-surgical rehabilitation, and paediatric developmental conditions. Each treatment plan is fully tailored to the patient's needs.
02. Does Neo-Step provide one-to-one sessions?
+
Yes. All patients at Neo-Step receive dedicated one-to-one sessions with Dr. Komal to ensure focused attention, proper guidance, and optimal recovery outcomes. We do not use a batch or group model.
03. How does online consultation work?
+
You WhatsApp or call to schedule your slot, share your reports and scans in advance, then have a live video consultation with Dr. Komal via WhatsApp, Zoom, or Google Meet. After the session, you receive a personalised home exercise program. Follow-up sessions are available to track progress.
04. How many sessions will I need?
+
It depends on your condition. Some patients like Bell's Palsy cases recover within 2 weeks. Neurological conditions like stroke may require 40–70+ sessions. Dr. Komal will give you a realistic timeline after your first assessment.
05. Is physiotherapy useful before surgery too?
+
Absolutely. Pre-operative physiotherapy helps prepare the body, build strength, and improve surgical outcomes. It often leads to faster and more complete post-surgical recovery. Dr. Komal offers both pre- and post-surgical rehabilitation programs.
06. How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book by calling 73873 23622, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling the booking form on this page. Our team will guide you through the assessment process and schedule your first session at a convenient time.
