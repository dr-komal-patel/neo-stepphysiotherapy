About Neo-Step

Pimple Saudagar's Trusted Physiotherapy Centre

Neo-Step is one of Pimple Saudagar's most comprehensive physiotherapy clinics, equipped with specialised tools for both neurological and orthopaedic rehabilitation. Led by Dr. Komal, our team delivers evidence-based, patient-first care.

Whether you're recovering from a stroke, managing chronic back pain, or rehabilitating after surgery — we create a personalised programme designed around your specific goals.

  • Full neuro rehabilitation suite — stroke, Bell's palsy, spinal cord injury
  • Advanced equipment: TENS, ultrasound, IFT and more
  • Pre & post-surgical physiotherapy & fracture rehab
  • Paediatric therapy: cerebral palsy, developmental delays
  • Personalised home exercise programmes

Our Specialisations

Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services

From neurological rehabilitation to sports injuries — we offer the full spectrum of physiotherapy care under one roof.

🧠

Neuro Physiotherapy

Specialist rehabilitation for stroke, traumatic brain injury, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, Bell's palsy, and spinal cord conditions.

🦴

Orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Effective treatment for back pain, neck pain, frozen shoulder, tennis elbow, knee pain, ligament injuries, and joint conditions.

⚽

Sports Injury Rehab

Return-to-sport programmes for sprains, muscle tears, tendon injuries, and overuse conditions — with functional movement retraining.

🏥

Post-Surgical Rehab

Structured recovery after joint replacements, fractures, spine surgery, and orthopaedic procedures to restore full function safely.

👶

Paediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, play-based therapy for children with cerebral palsy, developmental delays, and motor skill challenges.

⚡

Electrotherapy & Modalities

TENS, ultrasound therapy, IFT, and hot/cold therapy to accelerate healing, reduce pain, and improve circulation.

🚶

Gait & Balance Training

Targeted programmes to improve walking patterns, coordination, and balance — especially for neurological patients and the elderly.

🤲

Hand Therapy

Rehabilitation for hand and wrist conditions, nerve injuries, post-fracture stiffness, and fine motor recovery.
